Featured Project

The canteen website

Problem statement
In college canteens, managing orders efficiently and minimizing wait times are crucial for enhancing student satisfaction. Traditional ordering methods often lead to long queues and slow service, particularly during peak hours.
Solution
This project introduces a streamlined, web-based QR code ordering system designed specifically for college canteens. By allowing students to place orders from anywhere on campus using their mobile devices, the system significantly reduces physical queues and wait times.
Key Features
1. User Interface.
2. Unique QR codes for every bill.
3. Dynamic data handling.
